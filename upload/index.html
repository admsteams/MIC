Who We Are

At Horizon Capital MIC, we make it simple for everyday investors to build steady wealth through real estate. As a Mortgage Investment Corporation, we pool investor funds and place them into carefully selected mortgages, all backed by Canadian property. Our focus is clear: security and consistent returns.
