Frequently Asked Questions

How are returns calculated?

Returns are calculated based on the annual interest rate for each investment option, compounded according to your selected frequency. The calculator provides an estimate of potential earnings, but actual returns may vary.

Are these returns guaranteed?

While our Fixed Return Investment offers a predetermined rate, Direct and Pooled investments returns are based on historical performance and are not guaranteed. All investments are secured by Canadian real estate mortgages.

What is the minimum investment?

The minimum investment varies by product. Direct Mortgage Investments typically start at $100,000, while Pooled and Fixed Return options may have lower minimums. Contact us for specific details.
