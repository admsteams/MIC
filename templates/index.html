    Who We Are

    Horizon Capital MIC

    At Horizon Capital MIC, we make it simple for everyday investors to build steady wealth through real estate. As a Mortgage Investment Corporation, we pool investor funds and place them into carefully selected mortgages, all backed by Canadian property. Our focus is clear: security and consistent returns.

    Investment Snapshot

    • Example Returns: 8-10% annually
    • Security: Every investment backed by Canadian real estate mortgages
    • Income Distribution: Interest collected from borrowers distributed back to investors

    With three investment styles — Direct Mortgage Investment, Pooled Mortgage Fund, and Fixed Return Investment — you can choose the approach that best fits your lifestyle and goals.

    Direct Mortgage Investment

    Your money goes directly into a specific mortgage deal. You earn the full interest paid by the borrower on that mortgage.

    Pooled Mortgage Fund

    Your money goes into a pool of mortgages managed by us. This spreads out the risk and gives you steady returns.

    Fixed Return Investment

    This option gives you certainty and predictability with a fixed return rate locked in for your term.
